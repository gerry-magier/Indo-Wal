Korowai Expedition in Papua

A demanding route for fit travelers. No show, no treehouse fantasy on demand.

This Korowai route is for travelers who do not want to tick Papua off as a quick sight. You need patience, fitness and the willingness to work with what is possible on the ground.

What this is

A private Korowai expedition via Agats, shaped by local agreements, weather, water routes and your ability to handle the conditions.

Physically demanding

Long days, heat, humidity, simple sleeping conditions, boats, trekking and waiting time are part of it.

Locally agreed

Visits only make sense when they are agreed locally and appropriate at the time of your journey.

Not staged

We do not sell performances, fake treehouse romance or people as scenery.

How the route can look

  • Travel independently to Papua and onward to Agats / Ewer.
  • From Agats onward, we handle the local coordination.
  • Continue toward Korowai areas by water and overland segments when conditions allow.
  • Village visits depend on permission, timing, route conditions and safety.
  • Return routing is planned with buffer because Papua is not predictable.

This fits if

  • you want a hard Papua journey, not an easy sightseeing loop.
  • you prefer honest expectations over big promises.
  • you can handle plans changing.
  • you need local coordination from Agats onward.
