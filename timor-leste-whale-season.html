Timor-Leste Whale Season

When a trip for blue whale sightings is actually worth planning.

The best time for blue whale sightings in Timor-Leste is October and November, when the whales move toward Australia through the water between Dili and Atauro.

Check expedition dates Swimming with blue whales

Best months

If blue whales are the reason for your trip, October and November are the months to look at first.

September

There can already be movement in September, but it is a less certain month for a focused blue whale trip.

October

One of the strongest months, with whales regularly moving through the deep water between Dili and Atauro.

November

Also a strong month. If you have several days, you give yourself more chances for calm conditions.

Where you go

The main area is off Dili and in the channel toward Atauro. Deep water comes close to shore here, which is one reason Timor-Leste is so interesting for blue whale encounters.

Conditions still matter every day: wind, swell, visibility, whale behavior and local decisions shape what is possible.

Our recommendation

  • Plan for October or November if possible.
  • Take several boat days if blue whales are the main reason for your trip.
  • Give yourself several days if the in-water experience matters.
  • Stay in Dili so the mornings on the water are simple.
