Why Timor-Leste?

Between September and November, Timor-Leste becomes one of the best places on Earth to encounter pygmy blue whales.

üêã About Blue Whales

  • Pygmy blue whales grow up to 25 meters long
  • Blue whales are the largest animals that have ever lived on Earth
  • They weigh between 40 to 70 tonnes
  • They migrate to Australian waters for feeding

üìç Timor-Leste in season

  • Approximately 600 whales are counted annually between September and November
  • We follow Timor-Leste's official watch and swim regulations
  • Respectful distance and calm behavior are non-negotiable
  • This is one of the most reliable blue whale encounters in the world
